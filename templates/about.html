About FoodShare

Our mission to reduce food waste and fight hunger

Who We Are

FoodShare is a non-profit platform that connects food donors (hotels, restaurants, events) with NGOs and community organizations that serve people in need. We believe that no good food should go to waste while people go hungry.

Founded in 2020, we have facilitated the donation of over 1 million meals, reducing food waste and feeding thousands of families across the country.

Our Vision

A world where surplus food is redirected to those who need it most, creating a sustainable and hunger-free community.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Reducing food waste helps the environment.
  • Community: Bringing donors and receivers together.
  • Safety: Ensuring all food is handled and transported safely.
  • Transparency: Clear communication and tracking of donations.
