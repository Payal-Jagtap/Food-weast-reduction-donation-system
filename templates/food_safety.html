Food Safety Guidelines

For Donors
  • Only donate food that is safe to eat and within its shelf life.
  • Keep hot food hot (above 60°C) and cold food cold (below 5°C).
  • Pack food securely to prevent contamination during transport.
  • Label food with preparation time and any allergens.
  • Follow local health department guidelines.
For NGOs
  • Inspect food upon pickup – if it looks/smells off, do not accept.
  • Transport food in clean, insulated containers.
  • Maintain temperature control during transport.
  • Distribute food as soon as possible, ideally within 2 hours.
  • Keep records of food distributed.
General Tips
  • Wash hands before handling food.
  • Use clean utensils and surfaces.
  • Separate raw and cooked foods.
  • When in doubt, throw it out.
